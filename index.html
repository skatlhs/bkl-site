What's barkly?

Barkly is a user to user app that helps people find their next pets. We show you listings of all types of pets, mostly dogs, in your area (state or province). We have listings from individual sellers, breeders, or shelters nearby. You can also find cats, reptiles, fish, small furry pets, birds, and toys, accessories, and equipment, for your pets as well.

It also works both ways. Want to list something? It takes less than 2 minutes to post something!

See How It Works

Finding Your Next Pet

With barkly, you can find pets, toys, and accessories located in your state/province.

You can find pets from individual sellers, breeders, or shelters nearby. We provide results based on your state or province.

We have over 60 different dog breeds categorized, as well as categories for fish, cats, reptiles, birds, and small furry pets.

Descriptive Information

Adding a new pet into your life can be difficult. We want to make sure you know what you're getting into.

We make sure our sellers enter all the necessary info on what they're listing.

We also made it easy to message our sellers! Just click the 'Contact Seller' button on their listing!
